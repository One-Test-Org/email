"Always there" feature..

Nikolaos Gkionis
March 15, 2025 6:26pm
Notified 3 people
This is an addition in the current "Saved for Offline" functionality. Instead of arbitrarily saving all the recent emails for offline viewing. Allow the user to chose which emails they want to keep, but let them keep images and attachments. 

The most frustrating thing I found was trying to get to my AirBnB key-lock instructions in a remote area. My phone was offline the email was not recent, and the "Aside" was not enough to hold on to it. The Villa owner/manager had decided to add images for the location of the locked key-holding in the email. Now, I could have screenshotted the email. But that is a workaround, and I think should not be the solution. You could add another expanding 'accordion' next to the "Reply later" & "Aside". But that is solving one problem whilst creating others. Namely, in a mobile view you have no room to have all 3 accordions side by side. 

Saying that, I realised that I use these 2 with the same idea, I want to keep them and take an action 'Later', this action might not be reply. As, I am not planning on replying to Adam, I just felt these were different enough, not to be on my 'Aside'. And even if their Sender,Subject and first Line is available 'Offline'.  The actual email is not.
